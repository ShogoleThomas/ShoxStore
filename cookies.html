What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and improve your browsing experience. Cookies can also provide information to the website owner about your preferences or activities.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly, such as enabling secure logins and navigating between pages.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, helping us to improve its performance.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember your preferences, such as language or region.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver ads that are more relevant to you and your interests.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party cookies from trusted providers, such as Google Analytics, to analyze website traffic and improve our services. These third parties may collect data about your online activities over time and across different websites.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but doing so may affect your user experience. For more information, visit All About Cookies.
